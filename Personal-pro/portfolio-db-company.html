Analytics Projects & Data Visulization

Project Information

  • Category: Data Analytics and Visualization
  • Client: Various

This multi-faceted project showcases a series of advanced data analytics and visualization implementations across finance, supply chain, and commercial sectors. The project demonstrates expertise in developing comprehensive dashboards and analytical tools that drive strategic decision-making and operational efficiency.

Key Features and Implementations

Finance

  • Financial Dashboards: Analyzed sales and rebates, providing crucial insights for financial planning and decision-making.

Supply Chain

  • Competitor Out of Stock Analysis and Dashboard: Provided weekly updates to monitor competitors' inventory levels, aiding strategic decisions.
  • Forecasting Dashboards: Enhanced demand planning and supply chain efficiency through predictive insights.
  • MAPE and BIAS Analysis: Continuously improved forecast accuracy and reduced biases.
  • Fill Rate and On-time Rate Monitoring: Tracked key supply chain performance metrics to maintain high service levels.

Commercial

  • Market Dashboards: Visualized market trends and competitive landscapes.
  • Daily Sales and Product Performance Analysis: Delivered detailed insights into sales performance and product metrics.
