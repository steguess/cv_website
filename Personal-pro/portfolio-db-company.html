Project Information

  • Category: Data Analytics and Visualization

The World Health Dashboard project focused on analyzing and visualizing global health data to provide insights into various health indicators. Using Google Analytics, the project featured interactive dashboards that illustrated key metrics such as global life expectancy, causes of death, and crude death rates. The dashboards included complex visualizations such as heatmaps, time series charts, and comparative bar charts to highlight trends and disparities in health outcomes across different regions. These visualizations enabled a comprehensive understanding of global health issues and facilitated informed decision-making for improving health policies and strategies. The project demonstrated expertise in data collection, statistical analysis, and advanced visualization techniques.
